Our Story

Just like Rome, Appyzie wasn't built in one day!

  • 2017

    An Idea

    Started with - Static Variable. A brand with a passion to build Apps and make people believe in Android.

  • 2018

    Created user base

    Created 10+ Android Applications, all hosted on PlayStore with a total of 5k+ downloads.

  • 2019

    Appian came into picture

    The team, individually, started learning Appian under an Appian partner to learn about enterprise apps.

  • 2020

    Stepping-Up

    Started working on the client projects of different geographies, and started training new talent on Appian.

  • 2021

    Started getting recognized

    Participated in the first ever live build challenge amongst the best 6 Appian developers around the world and was the runner up.

  • 2022

    Greater Heights

    Served 10+ clients, 15+ projects & 6+ domains. Started educating people world-wide with Appianspace.

  • 2023

    Inception of Appyzie!

    And here we are, with all the knowledge, skill and experience we've gathered, over the last 6 years, and finally putting it to work.

Awesomeness begin here. Are you ready?
