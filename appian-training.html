Appian Training FAQ

Do we have any experience in Training?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience in training individuals on Appian. With over 4 years of dedicated training expertise, we have successfully trained a diverse range of participants, including both freshers and experienced professionals. Our proven training programs have equipped numerous resources with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in working with the Appian platform. You can trust us to provide high-quality training that prepares individuals to be project ready. For instance, you can take a look at appianspace.com, a blog started by our trainers.

What is certification ready?

"Certification ready" means that upon completing our training program, you will possess the theoretical and practical knowledge necessary to confidently pass the Appian Associate Developer exam. Our comprehensive training equips you with the skills and understanding required to excel in the certification process, validating your expertise in Appian development. We ensure you are well-prepared to achieve your certification goals and start your Appian career journey as a successfully certified developer.

Can I apply as an Individual?

Why not! We welcome individual applicants who are eager to receive training on Appian. Our training program equips you with the skills and knowledge needed to kickstart your career in the IT industry. Upon successful completion, you can unlock exciting opportunities with top companies such as Accenture, American Express, Cognizant, Infosys, TCS, PwC, EY, and more.

What is the training plan?

Our training plans are designed to cater to diverse needs. We offer flexible options including classroom sessions, virtual sessions, and recorded sessions to accommodate your specific requirements. Each plan is equally effective and comes with its own set of advantages. Whether you prefer face-to-face interaction, remote learning, or self-paced study, we have the perfect training plan for you. Rest assured, all our sessions are designed to deliver excellent results and provide you with a comprehensive learning experience.

What all in Appian is covered?

Our training plans vary based on the type of plan you choose. However, rest assured that even our most basic plan includes comprehensive learning of all the essential Appian objects and concepts. We are committed to ensuring you are "Certification Ready" and equipped to clear the associate developer certificate. For specific details about what is covered in your chosen plan, we invite you to contact us today. Our team will provide you with the information you need to make an informed decision about your training journey with us.

What are the plans and pricing?

Our pricing for corporates is tailored to meet your specific needs and varies based on the type of plan you choose. We understand that each organization has unique requirements, and we are committed to providing you with a customized plan that best suits your needs. Contact us today, and our team will work closely with you to create the perfect plan with competitive pricing that aligns with your training goals and budget.
