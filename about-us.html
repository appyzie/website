When was Appyzie founded?

Appyzie, formerly known as Static Variable, embarked on its journey in 2017 with a focus on crafting exceptional Android apps. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded our horizons. In 2023, we transformed into Appyzie and officially registered as a Private Limited company, solidifying our commitment to delivering innovative solutions.
